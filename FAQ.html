Frequently Asked Questions

General FAQs:


  • Who can enroll in our courses?

    • Our courses are designed for individuals of all levels, from beginners to experienced professionals. No prior programming experience is required for introductory courses.


  • What programming languages are taught in your courses?

    • We teach a variety of programming languages including Python, Java, JavaScript, C++, and more, depending on the course.


  • Are your courses self-paced or instructor-led?

    • All our courses are self-paced. However, you can ask your doubts by filling up a form.


  • Do you offer certifications upon course completion?

    • Yes, we provide certificates of completion for all our courses, showcasing your new skills to potential employers.


Course-specific FAQs:


  • How can I enroll in your course?

    • To enroll, simply visit our website, browse the course catalog, and follow the enrollment instructions provided.


  • What are the prerequisites for specific courses?

    • Prerequisites vary by course. Basic computer literacy is typically recommended for our courses.


  • What is the duration of each course?

    • Course durations vary depending on the complexity and depth of the material. All our courses are self-paced. Most courses can take from a few weeks to a few months to complete.


  • Do you offer access to course materials after completion?

    • Yes, course materials are downloadable for future reference and review.


Miscellaneous FAQs:


  • How can I provide feedback on the courses?

    • We welcome your feedback! Please use our online feedback form or email us directly with your comments and suggestions.


  • Do you provide career guidance or job placement assistance?

    • No, at this moment we don't provide any career guidance or job placement assistance.


  • Can I upgrade to more advanced courses after completion?

    • Absolutely! We offer a range of advanced courses to further develop your skills and expertise.
